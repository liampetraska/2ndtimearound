Our Philosophy

2nd Time Around is an upscale consignment shop located in Okemos, MI. We believe that fashion doesn't have to cost an arm and a leg. Our selection is constantly changing so come back often and see what's new!

Need something for everyone? We carry clothing for every member of the family from men's suits to women's dresses and everything in between.

Making Consignment Chic

We accept all sorts of used clothing from that dress you wore once to your favorite pair of jeans! We understand that we both want to sell your clothing so we agree on a price for all of your items. That way you walk away happy!

While we are happy to look at any types of clothing, be sure to visit the requirements outlined on the Consignment page.
